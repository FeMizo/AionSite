## Design Context

### Users
Small businesses, local entrepreneurs, startups, and agencies in Latin America (primarily Mexico) who need professional web presence. They may not be technically sophisticated but understand the value of a premium, high-converting website. They arrive via word of mouth or WhatsApp and convert through a single CTA.

### Brand Personality
Precise, fast, unique. AionSite should feel like a high-performance studio — not a freelancer, not a generic corporate agency. Sharp and confident without being cold.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark theme (established, non-negotiable). Blue is the primary accent — can be refined within the blue/indigo/sky/cyan family but no warm second accent needed. Refined restraint over maximalism. No reference sites. Anti-references: generic AI/template aesthetic, purple-blue gradient slop.

### Design Principles
1. Precision over decoration — every color element earns its place
2. Speed signals through sharp contrast and clean hierarchy, not busyness
3. Blue hierarchy: one primary blue, cool-hue variants for depth and service differentiation
4. WCAG AA minimum for all text color combinations
5. Unique = unexpected precision in ordinary places (process numbers, section landmarks, icon color differentiation)
